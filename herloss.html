Her Loss

"Her Loss" is a collaborative album by Drake and 21 Savage, released on October 28, 2022. The album showcases a mix of hip-hop and rap styles, exploring themes of wealth, fame, and relationships. The chemistry between Drake and 21 Savage is evident throughout the project, delivering catchy tracks with strong lyrical content.
