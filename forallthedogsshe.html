For All the Dogs: Scary Hours Edition

"Drake’s For All The Dogs (Scary Hours Edition), his eighth studio album, was released on November 17, 2023, as a deluxe version of For All The Dogs. Featuring six additional tracks, the project blends introspection, storytelling, and collaborations, including a standout track with J. Cole, "Evil Ways." With themes ranging from heartfelt to bold, the album showcases Drake’s versatility and cements his place in modern music."
