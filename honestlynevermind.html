Honestly Nevermind

"Honestly, Nevermind" is Drake's seventh studio album, released on June 17, 2022. It marks a departure from his usual rap and R&B style, leaning heavily into house and dance music. The album explores themes of heartbreak, longing, and self-reflection, showcasing a different side of Drake's artistry.
