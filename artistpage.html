Drake

Drake, born Aubrey Drake Graham in 1986, is a Canadian rapper, singer, and actor who first gained fame on Degrassi: The Next Generation. He transitioned to music with his breakthrough mixtape So Far Gone (2009) and rose to global stardom with hits like "Best I Ever Had" and albums such as Take Care (2011) and Views (2016). Known for blending rap, R&B, and pop, Drake has broken streaming records, won multiple Grammy Awards, and established a significant cultural influence. He also founded the OVO Sound label and is involved in various business ventures, cementing his place as one of the most influential artists of his generation.
